User Story

Play as Pepe, an ordinary Mexican guy having an extraordinary bad chicken day. Out of nowhere, flocks of chickens—from tiny troublemakers to giant feathered bosses—have gone berserk and started attacking people! Armed only with his abuela’s legendary Tabasco sauce bottles, Pepe must spice up the battlefield, tame the rampaging chickens, and save the world one hot splash at a time.
